# Branding Guide

## Brand Name

Chiq-N-Grill

## Tagline

Bold Chicken. Smooth Vibes. Accra’s Grill Experience.

## Brand Personality

- Premium
- Warm
- Urban
- Flavorful
- Confident
- Relaxed
- Social

## Visual Direction

The design should feel like a luxury Accra grill house. Avoid default template cards, generic gradients, fake restaurant copy, and overused AI-style sections.

## Color Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---:|---|
| Charcoal Black | `#111111` | Main background, footer, dark cards |
| Smoked Brown | `#3A2418` | Warm section backgrounds |
| Burnt Orange | `#D96B27` | Primary CTA, highlights, active states |
| Flame Gold | `#F5B041` | Premium accent, dividers, badges |
| Cream | `#FFF4E6` | Background, text contrast |
| Soft Gray | `#E8E2D8` | Borders, subtle cards |

## Typography

Recommended heading fonts:

- Playfair Display
- Cormorant Garamond
- Bodoni Moda

Recommended body fonts:

- Inter
- Poppins
- Montserrat

## Photography Direction

Use real food and restaurant images wherever possible.

Preferred shots:

- Close-up chicken texture
- Jollof rice and chicken plate
- Herb butter rice plate
- Fries and sauce
- Interior seating
- Drinks and hangout mood
- Night/warm lighting shots

Avoid:

- Fake AI food that looks plastic
- Random stock photos with non-Ghanaian food context
- Overly glossy, unrealistic plates

## Copy Tone

Short, confident, appetizing, and local.

Examples:

- Made hot. Served bold.
- Chicken with character.
- Come for the chicken. Stay for the vibe.
- Accra flavor, grill-house energy.
- Flavor that deserves a second visit.

## UI Style

- Dark luxury base
- Warm flame accents
- Rounded cards but not childish
- Asymmetric layouts
- Editorial food layout
- App-like mobile ordering flow
- Subtle micro-interactions
- No heavy animation spam
